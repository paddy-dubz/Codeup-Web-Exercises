What made you want to become a software developer?

After college it was difficult finding work in the design industry that would support my family, so I sold my soul and became a marketing and sales associate for an intangible services company. The money was good and the work was easy, but it wasn't anywhere near fulfilling enough. I felt myself withering away on the inside and after 6 years finally decided enough was enough; I was going to pursue my childhood dream of designing and creating websites.
